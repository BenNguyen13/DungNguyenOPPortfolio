About Me

I’m Nguyen Vu Tien Dung, or also known as Ben, an 18 year old aspiring UX designer, currently developing my skills through freelance jobs and higher education.
I’m skilled in using design apps such as Figma and AdobeXD .

While being an UX designer, I am also passionate in filming and video editing in which I use my free time editing videos through Davinci Resolve 18 , in which I’m quite proficient in.

I‘m friendly and honest individual that believes keeping active is important. I do this with my hobbies such as sports like football and cycling. From playing team sports throughout my life I have learnt how to be effective in a team which helped me during my school life and will in the future to fit well into team environment.

I took upon UXD course in the Netherlands as I believe it is the first step towards learning about different aspects and cultural boundries. It is very interesting to study how people from different parts of the world think and to how we should design for them. These factors are what motivate me to strive to become a relatable, smart, and successful designer. I will strive to share my design style of mini-malistic, intricate, and effect focused style as much as possible while also satisfying people who come across it.
